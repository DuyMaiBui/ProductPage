Your Privacy Matters: We respect your right to control your personal data. This page explains how to request deletion of your data from Unscrew Factory.

How to Request Data Deletion

Method 1: Email Request (Recommended)

  1. Send an email to: duymaib@the1studio.org
  2. Use subject line: Data Deletion Request - Unscrew Factory
  3. Include in your email:
    • Your Facebook ID or the email associated with your account
    • Your in-game username (if applicable)
    • Approximate date of account creation
    • Reason for deletion (optional)
  4. You will receive confirmation within 48 hours

Method 2: In-Game Request

  1. Open Unscrew Factory game
  2. Go to Settings menu
  3. Select "Account" or "Privacy"
  4. Tap "Delete My Data"
  5. Follow the confirmation steps

What Data Will Be Deleted

When you request data deletion, the following information will be permanently removed:

  • Account Information: Your Facebook ID, profile name, and profile picture
  • Game Progress: All levels completed, scores, and achievements
  • Purchase History: Records of any in-app purchases (refunds not included)
  • Social Data: Friend connections, leaderboard entries, and shared content
  • Analytics Data: Usage statistics and gameplay metrics linked to your account
  • Communication Preferences: Email preferences and notification settings
  • Device Information: Linked device IDs and technical data

What Data May Be Retained

Certain data may be retained for legitimate business purposes:

  • Transaction Records: Required for financial and tax compliance (anonymized)
  • Legal Obligations: Data required to be kept by law
  • Fraud Prevention: Anonymized data for security purposes
  • Aggregated Analytics: Non-identifiable statistical data

Deletion Timeline

Expected Processing Time:

  • Acknowledgment: Within 48 hours of request
  • Verification: 1-3 business days
  • Deletion Process: 7-14 business days
  • Complete Removal: Up to 30 days for all systems
  • Backup Removal: Up to 90 days for backup systems

Important Considerations

Warning: Data deletion is permanent and cannot be reversed. Please consider the following before requesting deletion:
  • All game progress will be permanently lost
  • Virtual items and currency cannot be recovered
  • You will lose access to any purchases or premium content
  • Friend connections and social features will be removed
  • You cannot restore your account after deletion

Alternative Options

Before requesting complete data deletion, you may want to consider:

  • Account Deactivation: Temporarily disable your account while keeping data
  • Data Export: Request a copy of your data before deletion
  • Partial Deletion: Remove specific types of data only
  • Privacy Settings: Adjust privacy controls instead of deletion

Data Export Request

You have the right to receive a copy of your data before deletion. To request data export:

  1. Email us at least 7 days before requesting deletion
  2. Use subject line: Data Export Request - Unscrew Factory
  3. We will provide your data in JSON or CSV format
  4. Data export typically takes 3-5 business days

Re-registration After Deletion

If you delete your data and later wish to use Unscrew Factory again:

  • You can create a new account
  • Previous progress cannot be restored
  • Previous purchases cannot be transferred
  • You will start as a new player

Your Rights Under Data Protection Laws

Depending on your location, you may have additional rights under laws such as:

  • GDPR (European Union): Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
  • CCPA (California): Right to delete personal information
  • LGPD (Brazil): Right to deletion of unnecessary data
  • Other Regional Laws: Various data protection rights

Verification Process

To protect your account, we may verify your identity before processing deletion:

  • Confirmation email to registered address
  • Verification of account details
  • Additional security questions if needed

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can I cancel my deletion request?

A: Yes, you can cancel within 7 days of your initial request by emailing us with subject "Cancel Deletion Request".

Q: Will deletion affect my Facebook account?

A: No, only data within Unscrew Factory is deleted. Your Facebook account remains unaffected.

Q: Can I delete data from specific time periods only?

A: We can only perform complete account deletion, not partial time-based deletion.

Q: What happens to my spot on leaderboards?

A: Your entries will be removed from all leaderboards upon deletion.

Q: Can someone else request deletion of my account?

A: No, only the account owner can request deletion after proper verification.

Legal Compliance

Our data deletion practices comply with applicable data protection regulations. We maintain records of deletion requests for legal compliance and audit purposes (without personal data).
